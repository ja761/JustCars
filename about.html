About Sian

  • Name: Sian Roadster
  • Company: Lamborgini
  • Type: Supercar
    •

    The Lamborghini Sian is not only the Italian marque's first production-bound hybrid but also its most powerful road car ever. That is some way to introduce your first supercar with an electric motor, but then, this is a manufacturer that is all about causing a stir in everything it does.
